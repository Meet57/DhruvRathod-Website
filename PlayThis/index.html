Playthis

On World Music Day, we turned the internet’s favourite lingo into playlists. We partnered with Twitter to create a unique engine that mapped all 3400 emojis across iOS and Android to Spotify playlists that would match the emojis. All a user had to do was tweet the hashtag #PlayThis followed by an emoji of their choice and get a Spotify playlist relevant to the emoji within a second.
